Pricing FAQs

Is there a minimum contract length?

No. All subscriptions are month-to-month after the initial setup. Cancel anytime.

What if I exceed my usage limits?

We monitor usage and contact you before any overages occur. Overage rates are reasonable and transparent - typically £0.05/execution or £0.50/AI operation.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. The only additional costs are optional add-on services (custom integrations, extra training) which you explicitly request.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

Do you offer discounts for annual prepay?

Yes - save 15% by paying annually in advance. Contact us for a custom quote.
