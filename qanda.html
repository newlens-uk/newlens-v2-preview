Technical & Implementation

What is self-hosted managed? Do I need to be technical?

Self-hosted managed means: You get a VPS from Hetzner or DigitalOcean (£10-20/month, we guide you through signup - takes 5 minutes). We SSH in, install n8n, configure security, build your workflows, and manage everything ongoing. You never touch code or servers.

Why this model? You own the VPS forever (can self-manage later if you want), lower monthly cost (you're not paying us for hosting markup), data sovereignty (your VPS, your location), full visibility (you have root access if curious).

Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Very likely, yes. We integrate with: Standard apps (Xero, HubSpot, Slack, Office 365, Google Workspace), custom APIs (if your system has an API, we can connect), legacy systems (screen scraping, file transfers when APIs don't exist), databases (direct connections when needed). n8n has 400+ pre-built integrations. During your assessment, we'll audit your tech stack and confirm compatibility.

What happens if an automation breaks?

We monitor 24/7 and catch most issues before you notice. Automated monitoring alerts us to failures, we diagnose root cause (API change? Data issue?), fix and deploy (usually within hours), then notify you of issue + resolution. Response times: Starter (48h), Core (24h), Growth (12h), Professional (4h). Plus, we build resilience into automations (retry logic, fallbacks, graceful failures). Most "breaks" are handled automatically.

Security & Compliance

Is our data safe? What about security?

Absolutely. Your VPS, your control: Data lives on your VPS (you choose location - UK options available), you have root access if needed. We secure it: SSL/TLS encryption, secure credential storage (encrypted vault), firewall configuration, regular security patches, SSH key authentication (no passwords). Monitoring: 24/7 uptime checks, automated alerts, daily backups. Self-hosted gives you maximum control - data never leaves your infrastructure.

Are you GDPR compliant?

Yes. UK-based company (data controller), your VPS can be UK data centre (you choose Hetzner/DigitalOcean location), Data Processing Agreements (DPA) provided, right to erasure/portability/access fully supported, privacy by design in all automations. We can also help you become more GDPR compliant - automations can enforce data retention policies, manage consent, and provide audit trails.

Who has access to our data?

Minimal, controlled access. Only engineers working on your project (typically Mat, the founder), access logged and auditable, NDA and confidentiality agreements in place, access via SSH key (secure). We only access when working on your workflows - no standing 24/7 access. Your VPS is yours - you can revoke our access anytime and keep running the automations yourself if you want.
