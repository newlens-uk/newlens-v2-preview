Everything you need to know about Newlens AI automation services.

Getting Started

What exactly does Newlens do?

We help UK businesses automate repetitive work using AI and intelligent workflows. Think of us as your automation partner - we assess your processes, build custom workflows, and manage the technology so you can focus on growing your business. Unlike DIY platforms, we handle everything from strategy to implementation to ongoing support.

How is Newlens different from Zapier or Make?

Great question! Zapier and Make are DIY platforms - you build automations yourself. Newlens is a full-service partner. We provide expert consulting (we design the automations for you), advanced AI capabilities (not just connecting apps), UK-based support and data hosting, ongoing optimization and monitoring, and custom integrations beyond standard app connectors. If you want a managed service with UK data residency, we're your solution.

Do I need technical knowledge to work with Newlens?

No! That's the point. We handle all the technical complexity. You just need to explain your business processes (you're the expert there), review and approve what we build, and use the automations (simple web interfaces, no coding). We translate business problems into technical solutions - you don't need to speak code.

How long does it take to get started?

Initial consultation: 30-60 minutes (book online)
AI Readiness Assessment: 1 week
First automation live: 2-4 weeks (depending on complexity)
Full implementation: 4-12 weeks (typical project)

We can move faster for urgent needs - our record is 3 days from kickoff to first automation running!

What size businesses do you work with?

We're built for UK SMEs - typically 10-500 employees across finance, legal, property, professional services, and healthcare. That said, we've helped solo consultants and also worked with divisions of larger enterprises. If you have repetitive processes eating your team's time, we can probably help.

Technical & Implementation

What technology does Newlens use?

We use best-in-class open-source and enterprise tools: n8n (automation platform), OpenAI/Anthropic/Google (AI models), PostgreSQL/Supabase (UK-hosted data), Docker/Kubernetes (infrastructure), and 400+ pre-built connectors plus custom APIs. You're not locked into proprietary technology - if you ever want to self-manage, you can.

Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Very likely, yes. We integrate with standard apps (Xero, HubSpot, Slack, Office 365, Google Workspace), custom APIs (if your system has an API, we can connect), legacy systems (screen scraping, RPA, file transfers when APIs don't exist), and databases (direct SQL connections). In 5+ years, we've only encountered 2 systems we couldn't integrate.

What if we already use Zapier/Make/Power Automate?

We can: Audit what you've built (find gaps, inefficiencies), Enhance your existing automations (add AI, improve logic), Migrate to a more powerful platform (if you've outgrown DIY), or Manage your current platform (if you want expert oversight). Many clients come to us when their DIY automations break or become too complex to maintain.

What happens if an automation breaks?

We monitor 24/7 and catch most issues before you notice. Automated monitoring alerts us to failures, we diagnose root cause, fix and deploy (usually within hours), then notify you of issue + resolution. SLAs: Bronze (48h), Silver (12h), Gold (4h with 24/7 emergency line). Plus, we build resilience into automations with retry logic, fallbacks, and graceful failures.

Security & Compliance

Is our data safe? What about security?

Absolutely. UK data residency (all data in UK data centres, GDPR-native), AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit, role-based access with MFA, SOC 2/ISO 27001 aligned, full audit logs, and Zero Trust architecture. Self-hosted clients get even more control - data never leaves your infrastructure.

Are you GDPR compliant?

Yes. UK-based company, UK data centres (London region), Data Processing Agreements (DPA) provided, right to erasure/portability/access fully supported, privacy by design in all automations, and regular data audits. We can also help you become more GDPR compliant - automations can enforce data retention policies, manage consent, and provide audit trails.

Can you handle sensitive data (financial, healthcare, legal)?

Yes, with appropriate controls. Financial data: PCI DSS controls available (tokenization, encrypted storage). Healthcare data: NHS Digital frameworks, DSPT compliance. Legal data: Solicitors' compliance, client confidentiality protections. HR data: Sensitive personal data handling. We assess sensitivity during discovery and apply appropriate security controls. Self-hosted deployments give you maximum control.

Who has access to our data?

Minimal, controlled access. Newlens-Hosted: Only engineers working on your project (2-3 people max), access logged and auditable, NDA and confidentiality agreements in place, access revoked when project complete. Self-Hosted: You control access completely. We can access only if you grant temporary credentials (for support). No standing access to your infrastructure. We also support data anonymization for development/testing.

What about AI security? Can AI leak our data?

We use AI safely: Data minimization (only send necessary context, not entire databases), Enterprise AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic) with data retention controls, Local AI option (for highly sensitive data, run models on your infrastructure - no data leaves your network), Prompt engineering to avoid data leakage, and Output validation before use. You can audit exactly what data is sent to AI models - full transparency.
