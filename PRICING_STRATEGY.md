# Newlens Pricing Strategy - February 2026

## Executive Summary

Three-tier approach:
1. **Self-Hosted** - Higher upfront, lower monthly (enterprise control)
2. **Newlens-Hosted** - Lower upfront, predictable monthly (managed service)
3. **Support Tiers** - Bronze/Silver/Gold (addon to either model)

---

## Model 1: Self-Hosted (Enterprise)

**Best for:** Organizations with IT teams, strict data residency, full control needs

### Initial Setup
- **Discovery & Assessment:** £4,995
  - 2-week engagement
  - Infrastructure audit
  - Security review
  - Architecture design

  - AI model integration

  - Documentation package

**Total Initial Investment:** £14,990 - £29,990
